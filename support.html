Privacy Policy

WYS2PDF is designed with privacy as a core principle.

  • All processing is performed locally on your Mac.
  • No personal data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • No advertising.
  • No user accounts.
  • No information is transmitted to external servers.

Generated PDF files remain entirely under your control and are stored only at the location you choose.

OCR

Optional OCR is performed locally using Apple's built-in Vision framework. No images or text leave your device.
