Frequently Asked Questions

Does WYS2PDF upload my data?

No. All processing, OCR recognition and PDF generation are performed locally on your Mac. No PDF files, URLs or document contents are transmitted to the developer.

Can I create searchable PDFs?

Yes. If OCR is enabled, the generated PDF contains searchable text.

Does WYS2PDF support login pages?

Yes. Cookies can be stored locally, so repeated logins are often unnecessary.

Which languages are supported?

  • German
  • English
  • French
  • Spanish

Where are my PDFs stored?

The PDF is saved to the location you selected. No copies are stored on external servers.

Privacy

WYS2PDF works completely locally on your Mac. The app does not collect analytics, usage statistics, advertising identifiers, tracking information or personal data.

For more information please see the Privacy Policy.
