WYS2PDF Privacy Policy

WYS2PDF converts web pages into searchable PDF documents directly on your Mac. All processing takes place locally on your device.

Local Processing

Web page rendering, OCR recognition and PDF generation are performed entirely on your Mac. No document contents, OCR text, URLs or generated PDF files are transmitted to the developer or to third parties.

No Tracking or Analytics

WYS2PDF does not collect usage statistics, analytics data, advertising identifiers or tracking information.

Network Access

The application only accesses web pages that you explicitly open. It does not communicate with developer servers or upload application data.

Local Storage

PDF files, workspaces, cookies and application settings are stored locally on your Mac. You remain in full control of these files and may delete them at any time.

Cookies

If enabled, cookies are stored locally to keep you signed in to supported web sites. Cookies are never transmitted to the developer.

Legal Notice

WYS2PDF creates PDF documents from web pages that you are able to display yourself. Please respect copyright, license agreements and the terms of use of the respective website.

The application is not intended to bypass paywalls, DRM systems, captchas or other technical access restrictions.
